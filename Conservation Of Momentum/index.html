Conservation Of Momentum¶

  • \[\Sigma p_{i}= \Sigma p _{f}\]
  • \[m_{1}u_{1}+m_{2}u_{2}= m_{1}v_{1}+ m_{2}v_{2}\]
  • p is momentum
  • v and d are velocity
  • m is mass
