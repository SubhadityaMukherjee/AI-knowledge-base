# Rotary Vector Drive (RV)
- A brand name for a speed reduction device that converts high speed low torque to low speed high torque, usually used on the major (larger) axis . See [Cyclo_Drive](Cyclo_Drive.md) and [Harmonic_Drive](Harmonic_Drive.md).
