# Clamp
- An [[End-effector.md|end-effector]] which serves as a pneumatic hand that controls the grasping and releasing of an object. Tactile, and feed-back [[Force.md|force]] sensors are used to manage the applied [[Force.md|force]] to the object by the clamp.
