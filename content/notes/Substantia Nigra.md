# Substantia Nigra
- This small region in the midbrain is part of the brain’s reward system. In [Parkinson’s disease](Parkinson%E2%80%99s%20Disease.md), the dopamine neurons in this region die off, leading to the disorder’s movement-related and cognitive symptoms.
