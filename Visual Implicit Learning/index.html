Visual Implicit Learning

  • Does ability to learn implicit dependencies correlate with ability to correctly predict the next word in speech?
  • Participants saw a sequence of colors light up on screen
  • They then had to reproduce it by clicking on the same sequence
  • Auditory Only Sentence Perception
  • 25 highly predictable and 25 zero-predictability sentences
  • acoustically degraded by processing them with a sinewave vocoder to 6 channels
  • Ability to pick up/learn statistical dependencies seems almost to be a new cognitive function
  • Shows a potential connection between sequence learning and language modelling ability
  • Learning simple dependencies is not so difficult it seems. But natural language has many dependencies at a distance
