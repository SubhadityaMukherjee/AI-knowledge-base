One Hot Encoding¶

  • Given \(A = {a_{1}, … , a_{k}}\)
  • Turn each \(a_{v}\) into k dim binary vector \(v_{v} \in {0,1}^{k}\) which is 0 everywhere execpt at position v
  • Symbolic input
  • k dim one hot vector
