# Covariance
- how much two random variables vary together
- ![[assets/Pasted image 20220617141519.png]]
- $$Cov(X,Y) = \frac{\Sigma(x_{i}- \bar x)(y_{i}- \bar y)}{N-1}$$
- $\bar x$ is the mean of x
