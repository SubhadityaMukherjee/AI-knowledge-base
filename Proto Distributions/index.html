  • [[Proto PDF]]
  • \[p(x|\theta) = \frac{1}{\int_{\mathbb{R}^{k}}p_{0}(x| \theta)dx}p_{0}(x|\theta)\]

  • \(\frac{1}{\int_{\mathbb{R}^{k}}p_{0}(x| \theta)dx}\) is a normalization so it integrates to 1
