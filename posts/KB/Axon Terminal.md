# [Axon](Axon.md.md) Terminal
- The very end of the [axon](Axon.md.md), where electrochemical signals are passed through the synapse to neighboring cells by means of neurotransmitters and other neurochemicals. A collection of axons coming from, or going to, a specific brain area may be called a white matter fiber tract.
