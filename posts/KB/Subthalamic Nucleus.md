# Subthalamic Nucleus
- A small brain structure, located in the [basal ganglia](Basal%20Ganglia.md), that plays an important role in coordinating movement. It is the most common target for neuromodulation techniques, like [deep brain stimulation](deep brain stimulation.md), to help diminish the symptoms of [Parkinson’s disease](Parkinson’s%20Disease.md).
