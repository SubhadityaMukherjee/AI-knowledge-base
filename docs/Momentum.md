# Momentum
- Momentum is the velocity of a body multiplied by its mass. A small [force](Force.md) can quickly stop an object with low momentum, but a large or prolonged [force](Force.md) is required to stop an object with high momentum.
- $$p = mv$$
- mass times velocity
