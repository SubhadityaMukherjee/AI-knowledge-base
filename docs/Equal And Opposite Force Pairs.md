# Equal And Opposite [[Force]] Pairs
- "When one body exerts a [[Force|force]] on a second body, the second body simultaneously exerts a [[Force|force]] equal in magnitude and opposite in direction on the first body."
- $$F_{1}= -F_{2}$$
