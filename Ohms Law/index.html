Ohms Law¶

- Potential difference = current x resistance  - \(\(V= IR\)\)  - Ohm's Law applied to the full circuit: Electromotive force = current x (sum of the circuit resistance and the internal resistance of the cell)  - \(\(EMF = I(R+r)\)\)
