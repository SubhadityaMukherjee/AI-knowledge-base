# Subthalamic Nucleus
- A small brain structure, located in the basal ganglia, that plays an important role in coordinating movement. It is the most common target for neuromodulation techniques, like deep brain stimulation, to help diminish the symptoms of [Parkinson’s disease](Parkinson’s%20Disease.md).
