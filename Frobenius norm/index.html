Frobenius Norm¶

  • The Frobenius norm, sometimes also called the Euclidean norm
  • Lp Regularization
  • When p = q = 2 for the \(L_{p,q}norm\), it is called the Frobenius norm or the Hilbert–Schmidt norm, though the latter term is used more frequently in the context of operators on (possibly infinite-dimensional) Hilbert space. $$ { |A|{\text{F}}={\sqrt {\sum {i}^{m}\sum {j}{n}|a_{ij}|{2}}}={\sqrt {\operatorname {trace} \left(A^{*}A\right)}}={\sqrt {\sum {i=1}^{\min{m,n}}\sigma _{i}^{2}(A)}},} $$
  • where \({ \sigma _{i}(A)}\) are the singular values of A
