# Brocas Area
- If this area is damaged, one may have difficulty moving the tongue or facial muscles to produce the sounds of speech. The person can still read and understand spoken [[language.md|language]] but has difficulty in speaking and writing
- Broca's [[aphasia.md|aphasia]]
- Discovered by French physician Paul Broca in the late 19th century, this small region in the left [[frontal lobe.md|frontal lobe]] has been linked to speech production.
