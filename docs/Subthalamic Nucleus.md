# Subthalamic Nucleus
- A small brain structure, located in the [[Basal Ganglia|basal ganglia]], that plays an important role in coordinating movement. It is the most common target for neuromodulation techniques, like deep brain stimulation, to help diminish the symptoms of [[Parkinson’s Disease|Parkinson’s disease]].
