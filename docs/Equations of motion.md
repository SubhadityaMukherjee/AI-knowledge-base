# Equations of Motion
- $\Delta x$ is displacement
- $\Delta t $ is time
- v is final velocity
- u is initial velocity
- a is acceleration
- $$v = u + a\Delta t$$
- $$\Delta x = u \Delta t + \frac{1}{2}a \Delta t^{2}$$
- $$\Delta x = \frac{1}{2}(v+u) \Delta t$$
- $$v^{2}= u^{2}+2a \Delta x$$
