Sample Correlation¶

  • A type of Correlation
  • \[r_{xy}= \frac{s_{xy}}{s_{x}s_{y}}\]
  • \(s_{x}\), \(s_y\) are the sample Standard Deviation
  • \(s_y\) is the sample Covariance
